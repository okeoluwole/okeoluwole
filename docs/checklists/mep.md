# MEP Review Checklist

| Item | Review Focus | Severity Guidance | Suggested Fix / Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| M1 | HVAC loads and zoning compliance with SBC 801 & client comfort standards | Critical if undersized | Recalculate loads, resize AHUs/FCUs, adjust zoning. |
| M2 | Fresh air rates meeting Civil Defense and ASHRAE 62.1 equivalents | Major if <90% required | Increase OA intake, reconfigure ducting, add sensors. |
| M3 | Electrical panel schedules aligned with SEC requirements and spare capacity | Major if overload | Rebalance circuits, provide spare breakers, update schedules. |
| M4 | Emergency power and life-safety circuits segregated | Critical if missing | Provide dedicated UPS/genset feeds, relabel panels. |
| M5 | Plumbing fixture count per SBC 403 and client ratios | Major if shortfall | Add fixtures or adjust gender distribution. |
| M6 | Drainage slopes and cleanouts accessible | Minor unless blocked | Re-route piping, add access panels. |
| M7 | Coordination of MEP services with ceiling/structure (clash-free) | Major if conflicts | Update coordination model, adjust elevations. |
| M8 | Controls and BMS integration per client standards | Minor unless missing critical points | Update points list, integrate with existing BMS. |
